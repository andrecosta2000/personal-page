§ 01 · How I can help Two ways to work together 02 / 02 modes

A specialized partner for the electronics around your idea.

I partner with companies developing novel technologies to design and build the electronics around them, from first concept through production handoff.

01
Mode A · Project-based

Project-based development

End-to-end ownership of an electronics project: circuit and PCB design, firmware, testing, and the path to manufacturing. For companies that need a specific deliverable, defined and shipped.

02
Mode B · Advisory

Technical advisor

Ongoing input on architecture, technology choices, and engineering decisions for teams without an in-house electronics function. For companies that need expertise in the room, not full-time staff, with access to a trusted network of specialists when a problem calls for it.

Operating principle

Good engineering is finding the simplest solution that works. I reach for complex tools when a problem demands them, not by default.

§ 03 · About The engineer behind the work Throughline
A note about the work

From microgrids to lunar testbeds: making ideas interact with the real world.

My passion is for developing solutions that make ideas a reality, taking something envisioned and let it interact with the real world. Microgrids, FPGA-accelerated computer vision, space hardware, consumer sensing: different domains, one throughline: how physical systems become reliable products.

I'm eager to develop the simplest, most ingenious solutions for whatever the field is. I also care deeply about the people side of the work, pushing the limits of what's possible through honest business and team relationships.

"I take on every project as my own. I'll only say yes to what I believe I can deliver."
